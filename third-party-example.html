Third Party Website Example

This page demonstrates how the AI Assistant chatbot integrates seamlessly into any website

This is a sample third-party website showing how easy it is to add the AI Assistant chatbot to your existing site. The chatbot appears as a floating button in the bottom-right corner and doesn't interfere with your website's design or functionality.

Look at the bottom-right corner of this page - you'll see a blue chat button with a lightning bolt icon. Click it to start chatting with the AI assistant!

Integration is Simple

To add this chatbot to your website, just include this single line of code before the closing </body> tag:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/embed.js"></script>

That's it! The chatbot will automatically appear and work perfectly with your existing website.
